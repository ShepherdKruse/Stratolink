# Stratolink Partnership Guidelines

## Open Source License

Stratolink software and hardware designs are licensed under the MIT License for open source and community use. This ensures maximum adoption and community benefit.

## Commercial Partnerships

For commercial partnerships, custom licensing terms may be available that include:

1. **Attribution Requirements**
   - Prominent credit in user-facing documentation
   - Logo placement in partner applications (where applicable)
   - Acknowledgment in press releases and marketing materials

2. **Partnership Benefits**
   - Technical support and collaboration
   - Co-marketing opportunities
   - Access to additional resources and documentation

3. **Contribution Expectations**
   - Contributions back to the open source project (where applicable)
   - Sharing of improvements and modifications
   - Support for the Stratolink mission

## License Compatibility

Partners using Stratolink under MIT License must:
- Include copyright notice: "Copyright (c) 2024 Stratolink (Nonprofit Organization)"
- Include full MIT License text
- Maintain attribution in distributed software

Partnership agreements may include additional terms beyond the base MIT License.
